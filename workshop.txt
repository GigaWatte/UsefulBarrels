description=    [*]Rubber Hose to take Gas from barrel
description=    [*]Funnel for pouring Gas into the barrel
description=    [*]Funnel may speedup pouring action
description=    [*]Refueling generator from the Barrel

description=    [*]Randomized initial fluid and amount

description=[h3] How to [/h3]
description=[h4] Base usage [/h4]
description=Please verify that all necessary sandbox variables are enabled.
description=Then, find a Pipe Wrench and any metal barrel in the game (there are only four types available).
description=You can move your new Useful Barrel anywhere you like. It will be empty at first — even if you later discover 750 liters of fuel inside.
description=With the Pipe Wrench in your inventory, right-click on the barrel and look for the "Uncap ..." option.
description=This will allow the barrel to store fluids and run the initial fluid filling procedure (if enabled).
description=[h4] Refuel generator [h4]
description=The generator must be within 2 tiles of the barrel.
description=The generator must be turned off and have free space for fuel.
description=There must be at least one barrel with at least 1 liter of fuel for the option to appear.
description=You must also have a Rubber Hose, if this is enabled in the sandbox settings.
description=[h4] Refuel vehicle [h4]
description=The vehicle must be within 4 tiles of the player.
description=The vehicle's engine must be turned off.
description=The vehicle must have a part with the "gastank" category, and the part’s containerType must contain the word "Gasoline" (this info is mostly for modders).
description=The gas tank must have free space.
description=The nearest barrel with gas must be within 4 tiles of the vehicle.
description=You must also have a Rubber Hose, if this is enabled in the sandbox settings.
description=
