description=[h2] Features [/h2]
description=[list]
description=    [*]Use your Pipe Wrench to open the lid of the barrel!
description=    [*]Store any fluid inside
description=    [*]The amount of liquid inside the barrel affects the encumbrance
description=    [*]Rubber Hose to take Gas from barrel
description=    [*]Funnel for pouring Gas into the barrel
description=    [*]Funnel may speedup pouring action
description=    [*]Refueling generator from the barrel
description=    [*]Refueling vehicle from the barrel. and vice versa.
description=    [*]Randomized initial fluid and amount
description=    [*]Sandbox vars to control mod behaviour as you want
description=    [*]Chuckleberry Finn's Mod Update and Alert System integration for ingame ChangeLog
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2] Notes [/h2]
description=[list]
description=    [*]Uses new FluidContainer system of B42
description=    [*]Is safe to add in existing saves as well as removing, FuidContainer will remain
description=    [*]Barrels are now less fragile! But anyway Walkers will break it pretty quickly
description=    [*]Gasoline does not evaporate from the barrels
description=    [*]Modded vehicles and trailers are supported if the vehicle has a part with the "gastank" category and the part containerType contains the word "Gasoline" (this information is mostly for modders)
description=    [*]Please! Any full 800L barrels in your characters pocket or car trunk is your responsibility, there is no proper way to haul them. For now at least.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2] How to [/h2]
description=[h3] Base usage [/h3]
description=[list]
description=    [*]Find a Pipe Wrench and any metal barrel in the game (there are only four types available)
description=    [*]Move it to the garage at your base
description=    [*]Right click on it and select "Uncap ..." option
description=    [*]Find a Rubber Hose and a Funnel, then place them on the barrel to enable fluid transfer
description=[/list]
description=[h3] Refuel generator [/h3]
description=[list]
description=    [*]The generator must be near the barrel with at least 1 liter of fuel
description=    [*]The generator must be turned off and have free space for fuel
description=    [*]Find a Rubber Hose if you haven't found it yet, then place it on the barrel (or keep it in your inventory)
description=    [*]Right click on the generator and select "Refuel From Barrel"
description=[/list]
description=[h3] Refuel vehicle [/h3]
description=[list]
description=    [*]The vehicle must be near the player
description=    [*]The vehicle's engine must be turned off
description=    [*]The gas tank must have free space
description=    [*]The barrel with gas must be near the vehicle
description=    [*]Find a Rubber Hose if you haven't found it yet, then place it on the barrel (or keep it in your inventory)
description=[/list]
